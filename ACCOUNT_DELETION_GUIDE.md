# How to Delete Your Account

This guide will walk you through the process of permanently deleting your AdsChat account.

## ⚠️ Important Information

Before proceeding with account deletion, please note:

- **This action is permanent and cannot be undone**
- All your personal data will be deleted from our servers
- Your chat history will be removed
- Your profile information (name, photo, status) will be deleted
- You will be logged out of the app immediately
- Your phone number will no longer be associated with this account

## Steps to Delete Your Account

### 1. Open Profile Settings
- Launch the AdsChat app on your device
- Navigate to your profile by tapping on your profile picture or menu icon
- Tap on **"Edit Profile"** or **"Profile Settings"**

### 2. Locate the Delete Account Button
- Scroll down to the bottom of the Profile Settings page
- You will see a red button labeled **"Delete Account"**

### 3. Confirm Account Deletion
- Tap on the **"Delete Account"** button
- A confirmation dialog will appear asking you to confirm your decision
- The dialog will display a message warning you that this action is permanent

### 4. Final Confirmation
- In the confirmation dialog, you will see two options:
  - **Cancel**: Returns you to the Profile Settings without deleting your account
  - **Delete**: Proceeds with permanent account deletion

### 5. Complete the Deletion Process
- Tap on the **"Delete"** button to proceed
- The app will display a loading indicator while processing your request
- You will see a message: "Deleting your account..."

### 6. Deletion Complete
- Once the deletion is complete, you will be automatically logged out
- The app will redirect you to the login screen
- A confirmation message will appear: "Account deleted successfully"

## What Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following information is permanently removed:

- ✅ User profile (name, photo, about/status)
- ✅ Phone number association
- ✅ Chat messages and conversations
- ✅ Contact lists
- ✅ Notifications
- ✅ Status updates
- ✅ All stored preferences and settings

## What to Do After Deletion

After your account is deleted:

1. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use AdsChat again
2. Your phone number can be used to create a new account in the future
3. Previous chat contacts will not be able to see your profile or message you

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the account deletion process:

- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Try restarting the app and attempting deletion again
- If the problem persists, contact our support team

## Alternatives to Account Deletion

Before deleting your account, consider these alternatives:

- **Take a break**: Simply log out without deleting your account
- **Update privacy settings**: Adjust who can see your profile and contact you
- **Clear chat history**: Delete specific conversations instead of your entire account
- **Change your status**: Update your about/status to indicate you're inactive
